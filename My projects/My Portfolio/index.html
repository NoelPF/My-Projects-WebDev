Pinkremover

A webite for those who hate pink

Pinkremover started out as an assignment we got in web development class. We had to design a website using a tool of our choice to help with a problem one of our classmates had. I got paired up with my friend Elsa, and her problem was simple, she hated the colour pink. This gave me the idea to make a website that is completely pink but can turn green on the press of a button.

I chose to make the website using google sites since that according to me was the easiest tool available. I started working and eventually, Pinkremover was a complete, small, jokesite that got a laugh out of the client and served it's purpose.

A quick explanation of who I am

Hi!
My name is Noel Persson Ferrera and I am a student at Amerikanska Gymnasiet Stockholm in Sweden. At the time I am writing this I have finished half of the first school year and I really enjoy it here. I am in the class Tk21 which is a technical class with focus on web development. But enough about school.

I am 16 years old and I live with my parents in Stockholm, Sweden. We have two pets, a cat and a dog. I also have a half sister but she has her own apartment now so I only see her on holidays or special occasions.

In my spare time I play video games, hang out with my friends, watch anime and play basketball. I am also an assistant leader in a church group where I attend every other Wednesday and Sunday. Lately I have also been getting a lot more into coding and programming, even though I am still quite a beginner.

When I first started out my code was cluttered and unorganized.
By spacing out the code like showed in the video it helped me understand more what was doing what,
and problemsolving became a lot easier.

I decided to challenge myself with the css for this website.
I already knew quite a bit before but I feel like I really improved
my general knowledge of the language

For my Tic Tac Toe game I decided to use a bit of javascript,
this was a nice refreshing challenge since I have never worked with the language before.
I am really proud of how it turned out and how well I managed to do it.

Self noting is something I discovered when working on a previous website I made.
By writing small titles for everything I get a much larger understanding of what is what,
and making edits in the code becomes a lot easier.

When making my Tic Tac Toe game I decided to make that entire code as a different file,
and then just copy it over to this website. Since it was my first time using javascript I felt it was safer doing it this way.
In the end it turned out great and I believe making it seperate made it a lot easier.
